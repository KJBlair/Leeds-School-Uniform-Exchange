LEEDS SCHOOL UNIFORM EXCHANGE

Supporting the development of a network of uniform reuse schemes across Leeds

About

Leeds School Uniform Exchange is a project developed by Zero Waste Leeds, supported by Leeds City Council and Leeds Community Foundation's Jimbo's Fund.

We’re building a movement to help Leeds become a Zero Waste City by 2030. As part of that we’ve launched a new project to support the development of a network of uniform reuse schemes across Leeds.
